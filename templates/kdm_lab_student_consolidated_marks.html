How Your Marks Are Calculated
  • Rubrics Marks (20): Based on your best performance across all KDM lab experiments
  • Script Marks (15): Theory assessments and assignments (uploaded by instructor)
  • Viva Marks (10): Automatically calculated from your best KDM lab internal quiz attempt (out of 10 marks)
  • Total Internal (25): Script Marks + Viva Marks
  • Attendance Marks (5): Class participation and attendance (uploaded by instructor)
  • Final Internal Marks (50): Rubrics + Total Internal + Attendance
