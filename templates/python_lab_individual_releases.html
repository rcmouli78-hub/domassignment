How Individual Releases Work
  • Global Release: When you globally release an experiment, all your students can access it.
  • Individual Release: You can grant access to specific students even when the experiment is globally locked.
  • Priority: If either global or individual release is active, the student can access the experiment.
  • Toggle Action: Click the switch to instantly toggle access for individual students.
